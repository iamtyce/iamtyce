So what exactly do I do?

I specialise purely in SASS / CSS3, HTML5 and responsive web design. I don't write backend, dynamic code or even Javascript.

Why? Specialists make the world a beautiful place and that extends to the web. They care about the little things and work with other specialists to create truly incredible products.

—

I'm currently the lead developer on a project to unify a major, well known brand through a dynamic, living styleguide that's consumable as a Node module.

I work with a team of talented developers and designers to identify reusable components and create robust modules to then share amongst the wider team.

—

I love what I do and the people I get to work with.
I learn new things daily to make me a faster, more efficient and productive developer.
